Engagement FAQ

Common questions about OT security assessments and methodology.

Do you support legacy Level 1 systems?

Yes. We specialize in safe asset discovery for legacy systems (e.g., PLC-5, SLC 500, S7-300). We use passive monitoring and OT-native protocols to ensure that inventorying does not disrupt live physical processes.

How do you handle sensitive plant data?

All engagements are governed by strict NDAs. We prioritize on-site analysis to keep data within your perimeter and can operate in completely air-gapped environments. For remote analysis, we provide encrypted transfer channels.

What compliance frameworks do you use?

Our methodology is primarily built on **ISA/IEC 62443** and **NIST SP 800-82**. We also map findings to sector-specific mandates like NERC CIP (Energy) and NIS2 (EU Manufacturing) where applicable.
