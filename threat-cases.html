Energy Purdue Level 1

TRITON / TRISIS Safety System Compromise

Attack Vector: Malicious workstation compromise leading to reprogramming of Triconex Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS).

Outcome: Plant shutdown.
Lesson: Segment SIS networks completely from DCS.

Energy Purdue Level 2 & 3

Ukraine Grid Attack (CRASHOVERRIDE)

Attack Vector: Spearphishing IT network, credential harvesting, remote access via VPN into OT SCADA systems.

Outcome: Mass power outage.
Lesson: MFA on all external remote access to OT.
[ View Full Case Study ]
Water Purdue Level 2

Oldsmar Water Plant Breach

Attack Vector: Exploitation of dormant TeamViewer installation with shared passwords on an HMI operator workstation.

Outcome: Chemical dosage altered (thwarted manually).
Lesson: Remove unauthorized remote access tools.
[ View Full Case Study ]
Energy Purdue Level 4

Colonial Pipeline Ransomware

Attack Vector: Compromised legacy VPN profile with single-factor authentication leading to IT billing system lockdown.

Outcome: Precautionary shutdown of OT pipeline.
Lesson: IT/OT interdependencies require resilient IDMZ.
