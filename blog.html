Knowledge Base FAQ

Common questions about our research and reporting methodology.

How often is new threat intel published?

We aim to publish technical deep-dives monthly. However, urgent security advisories regarding zero-day exploits or active nation-state campaigns affecting OT assets are published immediately as verified data becomes available.

Are these articles based on real-world incidents?

Yes. All **Case Study** and **Threat Intel** posts are synthesized from public disclosures, ICS-CERT advisories, and de-identified engagement findings. We ensure 100% client confidentiality by sanitizing all proprietary architectural details.

Can I request a technical deep-dive on a specific PLC/SCADA system?

Absolutely. We prioritize research that provides the highest defensive value to the ICS community. If you have a specific protocol or vendor platform you'd like us to analyze, please submit a request via our **Contact** page.
